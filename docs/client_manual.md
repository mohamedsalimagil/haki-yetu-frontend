# Haki Yetu - Client User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to Haki Yetu. This guide explains how clients can register, find legal experts, and book consultations.

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration
1. Navigate to the **Home Page**.
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Register"**.
3. Select **"I am a Client"**.
4. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password).
5. **Note:** You must verify your email before booking (if enabled).

### Login
1. Click **"Login"** in the top navigation.
2. Enter your credentials.
3. Upon success, you will be redirected to the **Client Dashboard**.

## 2. Using the Dashboard
The **Client Dashboard** is your command center. From here you can:
* View upcoming appointments.
* Search for lawyers.
* Access your messages.

## 3. Booking a Lawyer (The Core Flow)

### Step 1: Find a Lawyer
1. Click **"Find a Lawyer"** in the sidebar.
2. Use the search bar to filter by **Name** or **Location** (e.g., "Nairobi").
3. Click **"View Profile"** on a lawyer card to see their details.

### Step 2: Book Consultation
1. On the Lawyer's profile, check their **Availability**.
2. Click the **"Book Consultation"** button.
3. Select a date and time slot.
4. Confirm your booking details.

## 4. Messaging
* To chat with a lawyer before or after booking, click the **Messages** icon.
* Select the lawyer from your contact list to open the secure chat room.

## Troubleshooting
* **Forgot Password?** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.
* **Chat not loading?** Ensure you have a stable internet connection and refresh the page.
